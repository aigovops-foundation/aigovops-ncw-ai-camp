Two futures

Maria's future, or Jake's future

Both are real. Both are happening in American schools this year. Which one North Central Washington gets is not automatic — it depends on the tools we choose and the rules we write.

M

Maria

8th grade · Wenatchee

Her AI tutor asks questions instead of giving answers. With Khanmigo she's up +23% in mastery and wants to be an engineer at Rocky Reach. She thinks she can — and she's keeping the receipts.

See how the day makes Maria's future
J

Jake

same school · no policy

A chatbot wrote his last seven essays. He shared a deepfake of his classmate Sara. His ability to spot fake news dropped −15.3% in four weeks — in a school with no rules and no one keeping the receipts.

See what went wrong, and why

The difference between them isn't talent or money. It's a human in the loop, a written policy, and the habit of keeping the receipts.
