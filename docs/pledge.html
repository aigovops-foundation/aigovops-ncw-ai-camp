Why this exists

Maria's future

An 8th grader in Wenatchee whose AI tutor asks questions instead of giving answers — and who is up 23% in mastery with Khanmigo. She wants to be an engineer. She thinks she can.

vs

Jake's future

A student who lets a chatbot write his essays — whose ability to spot fake news drops 15.3%, in a school with no policy and no one keeping the receipts.

Two stories. Both real. Both happening in American schools this year. This pledge exists because whether North Central Washington gets Maria's future or Jake's future is not automatic — it depends on the tools we choose, the rules we write, and whether a human stays in the loop. We're signing for Maria's future, together.
