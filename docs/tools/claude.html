What it does

Claude is Anthropic's AI assistant, prized for nuanced writing, careful reasoning, and handling very long documents. It offers a free web tier, with Pro and Max adding higher usage, larger projects, and access to the most capable models. Features like Projects and Artifacts make it strong for drafting and iterating on documents. Many educators favor its measured, less-hyped tone.

Best for, in an NCW context

  • Educators: workshop a sensitive parent email until the tone is exactly right, or summarize a long research article.
  • SMB owners: draft a thoughtful policy doc, refine a contract summary, or organize a messy brain-dump into a plan.
  • Community leaders: write a careful public statement, then use the two-eyes rule before it goes out.

⚠️ The Data Cost of “Free”

Remember: when a tool is free, you’re often paying with your data.

Free AI tools generally train on your prompts, your uploads, your edits, and your usage patterns. That’s the trade. Before pasting in customer info, student records, IEP drafts, or anything else you wouldn’t email to a stranger — flip to a paid tier (which usually offers a “do not train on my data” toggle), use the tool’s enterprise tenant if your org has one, or use a free tier with non-sensitive content only.

The AiGovOps 3 Rules apply here

  • 🚫Never paste private data into a free tool

  • 📂Keep the receipts
