## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

Each mini-camp section contains four parts:

| Section | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **EN Narration** | English voice-over, 140–200 words, production-ready |
| **ES Narración** | Mexican Spanish cultural adaptation (NOT literal translation), same length |
| **Voice & Avatar Guidance** | ElevenLabs voice name + settings, Synthesia avatar slug, pacing notes |
| **Storyboard** | 8–12 shots: timecode · visual description · on-screen text · narration line · Gamma prompt |

**Global style rules:**
- Every video opens with a named character in a vivid 5-second scene — no title cards, no logos until the end
- Music energy curve: quiet opening → building momentum → warm landing (same arc as the NCW trailer)
- Every video ends identically: CTA card with pledge URL
- Spanish: Mexican Spanish throughout. "Usted" for adults. "Tú" for children. Names preserved from English versions where they are real NCW names.
- Gamma style: photorealistic documentary, warm amber + sage green + river blue palette, no corporate stock-photo look

**CTA — English (every video):**

**CTA — Spanish (every video):**
